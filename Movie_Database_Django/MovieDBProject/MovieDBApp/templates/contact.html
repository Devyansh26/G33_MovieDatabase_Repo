Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account on MovieDB?

Creating an account is simple! Click on the "Register" button in the top-right corner of the page. Fill in your details including your name, email, and create a password. After registration, you'll be able to access all features including creating watchlists, rating movies, and leaving reviews.

How can I add movies to my favorites list?

To add a movie to your favorites, simply click the heart icon on any movie's page. You must be logged in to use this feature. You can view and manage your favorites from your profile page.

Can I suggest a movie that's not in the database?

Yes! If you notice a movie missing from our database, you can use the contact form to suggest it. Please include the movie's title, release year, and any other relevant information. Our team will review your suggestion and add it to our database if it meets our criteria.

How do I report incorrect movie information?

If you find any incorrect information about a movie, please use the contact form to report it. Include the movie title, the incorrect information, and the correct information. Our team will verify and update the details as soon as possible.

How can I leave a review for a movie?

To leave a review, you need to be logged in. Navigate to the movie's page and scroll down to the reviews section. Click on "Write a Review" and share your thoughts about the movie. You can also rate the movie using our star rating system.

Is MovieDB free to use?

Yes, MovieDB is completely free to use! All our basic features including browsing movies, creating watchlists, and leaving reviews are available at no cost. We may introduce premium features in the future, but our core services will always remain free.

How can I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and we'll send you instructions to reset your password. Make sure to check your spam folder if you don't receive the email.

How do I update my profile information?

To update your profile, log in and click on your username in the top-right corner. Select "Profile" from the dropdown menu. Here you can update your profile picture, email, and other personal information. Don't forget to save your changes!
